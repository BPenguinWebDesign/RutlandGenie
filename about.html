About Me

I am a Home & Internet Tutor for Rutland and the surrounding areas. I’ve been teaching for over 26 years.

I am a qualified teacher and home tutor with over 26 years of teaching experience, offering tuition in Maths, Biology, Physics and Chemistry to GCSE level and Maths to AS level and Biology to A level.

I also provide tuition in all subjects at Key Stage 1, 2 and 3 including music and French, and can help with school entrance exam preparation. I am fun, enthusiastic and creative, and have experience with a variety of special needs issues such as ADHD, dyslexia, dyspraxia, ASD and anxiety.

I am a bright, enthusiastic and dedicated teacher and tutor with over 26 years of teaching experience. I have excellent interpersonal and communication skills, and aim to inspire, motivate and encourage my students. I have a warm and friendly manner and am sensitive and understanding. I have high organizational capabilities, paying attention to detail. I am determined and decisive and use my initiative to meet and resolve challenges. I am diligent and conscientious with high personal standards. I am reliable and dependable and enjoy completing tasks well. I am technically competent; can touch type, and am an experienced user of PCs and Macs.
